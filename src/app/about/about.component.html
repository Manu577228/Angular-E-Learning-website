Diversification of Content Formats:
Elearning had evolved beyond traditional text-based courses to include a variety of multimedia formats such as videos, interactive simulations, podcasts, and virtual reality experiences. This shift aimed to enhance engagement.

Personalization and Adaptive Learning:
Advances in technology allowed for more personalized learning experiences. Adaptive learning platforms used data and analytics to tailor course content and pacing to individual student needs, improving learning outcomes.

Corporate Training and Upskilling:
Organizations recognized the value of eLearning for employee training and development. Elearning platforms were used to deliver training programs that focused on enhancing skills and maintaining a competitive workforce.
